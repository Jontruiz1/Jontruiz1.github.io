ABOUT

This organization aims to help computer science, IS, and IT majors/minors or those interested develop useful programming techniques. The club meets once a week to discuss different white board problems, coding experiences, and long term project goals. White board problems are typically used within programming interviews and are helpful to go through to prepare for the workforce. Additionally, we will talk about different coding experiences we have had. This will allow the older and more experienced members to share what they have done with the underclassmen. Also, the club will focus on a few long term projects to teach students about combining more than one IDE as well as collaboration through Github. Overall, this club will focus on working together with fellow students to learn about new and older programming techniques in order to be more marketable in the job market.
